The new OZET.US

Posted on March 05, 2011

In honor of OZET Day 2011 (that's March 6, the date of the first OZET performance), we've put together a new site for you all. We hope you like it.

While you're here, take a look at the short Alberts I-V "teaser" video we put together. If you weren't able to make it down to HERE in August, it should give you a good idea of how our latest OZET piece came out.

We've also started posting transmissions, short experimental pieces (all musical so far), to the site. Check back, as we'll be posting more of these on a regular basis.

And if you're interested in keeping up with all the OZET news, come to our facebook page and follow us on Twitter: @the_ozet.

Thanks, as always, for being part of OZET.
